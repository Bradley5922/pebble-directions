## Description (paste this)

Directions is a fast, simple navigation app for your wrist.

Pick how you're travelling, speak your destination, and get clear step-by-step directions on your Pebble — for driving, cycling, public transport, and walking.

★ Real public transport
Public transport now works across the UK and worldwide — the London Underground, Manchester's Bee Network trams, National Rail, buses and trams. Transit journeys include turn-by-turn walking directions all the way to and from the stop, so you're never left guessing outside the station.

★ Features
• Four travel modes: drive, cycle, public transport, and walk
• Turn-by-turn directions with the distance shown on every step
• Just speak your destination — no typing
• Save up to five favourite places (like "home" or "work") and simply say the name
• Automatic step advancement for walking and cycling routes (beta)
• Clear on-watch messages when something goes wrong, so you always know why

★ One-time setup (required)
Directions uses your own free Google Maps Platform API key. This keeps the app free with no shared usage limits — it takes a couple of minutes to set up. Full step-by-step instructions:
https://github.com/Bradley5922/pebble-directions#setting-up-your-google-api-key

Then open the app's settings on your phone, paste in your key, and you're ready to go.

Directions data is provided by the Google Directions API.
